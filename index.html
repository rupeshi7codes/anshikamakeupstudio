ABOUT US

Our Beauty Journey

Anshika Makeup Studio and Academy is your one-stop destination for all things beauty! Led by expert makeup faculty Anshika Sah, we empower aspiring artists with industry-ready skills while pampering clients with personalized beauty solutions.

Whether you're here to learn, transform, or simply treat yourself, we're dedicated to making you feel confident and beautiful—inside and out.

Meet Anshika Sah

Founder & Lead Makeup Artist

With over 8 years of experience in the beauty industry, Anshika has transformed thousands of clients and trained hundreds of aspiring makeup artists. Her work has been featured in Bridal Asia Magazine, and she's known for her signature "natural glam" style that enhances your unique features.

Certifications: CIDESCO | VLCC | Makeup Forever Academy
