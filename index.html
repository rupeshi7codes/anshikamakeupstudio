ABOUT US

Our Beauty Journey

Anshika Makeup Studio and Academy is your one-stop destination for all things beauty! We specialize in professional makeup courses, beautician training, and a wide range of salon services including facials, clean-ups, waxing, threading, and more. Led by expert makeup faculty Anshika Sah, our academy empowers aspiring artists with industry-ready skills while our studio pampers clients with personalized beauty solutions.

Whether you’re here to learn, transform, or simply treat yourself, we’re dedicated to making you feel confident and beautiful—inside and out. Book your appointment or join our courses today!

Meet Anshika Sah

Founder & Lead Makeup Artist

With over 8 years of experience in the beauty industry, Anshika has transformed thousands of clients and trained hundreds of aspiring makeup artists. Her work has been featured in Bridal Asia Magazine, and she's known for her signature "natural glam" style that enhances your unique features.

Certifications: CIDESCO | VLCC | Makeup Forever Academy
